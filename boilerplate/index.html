Literacy & Education

Literacy rates can be linked to factors such as employment rates, income, incarceration, and health. It goes beyond reading a book. It can determine how individuals will socially interact throughout their lives, having effects on emotional and intellectual development. Empowering children with the ability to read and write sets them up to make informed decisions later in life.
